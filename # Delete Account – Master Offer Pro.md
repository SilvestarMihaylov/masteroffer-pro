# Delete Account – Master Offer Pro

**Last updated:** 2026

Master Offer Pro allows users to permanently delete their account directly from within the application.

---

## How to Delete Your Account

1. Open **Master Offer Pro**.
2. Log in with your registered account.
3. Navigate to **Settings**.
4. Select **Delete Account**.
5. Confirm the deletion request.

Once confirmed, the deletion process is irreversible.

---

## What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

* Your Firebase Authentication account (email and login credentials)
* All offers created by you
* Client information
* Project data
* Any other content associated with your user profile

---

## What Data May Be Retained

Some data may be retained where required:

* Anonymous analytics data (not linked to your identity)
* Crash reports collected prior to deletion (non-personal)
* Subscription and purchase records managed by Google Play (handled by Google, not by the app developer)

---

## Subscription Notice

Deleting your account does **not automatically cancel an active subscription**.

If you have an active subscription, you must cancel it through your **Google Play account**:

Google Play → Payments & Subscriptions → Subscriptions
