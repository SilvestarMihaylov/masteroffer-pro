# Privacy Policy – Master Offer Pro

**Last updated:** 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected and used in Master Offer Pro.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

Depending on usage, the app may collect:
- Offer and project data
- Client information entered by the user
- Anonymous usage and analytics data
- Subscription status

Authentication methods:
- Anonymous sign-in
- Google Sign-In (optional)

---

## 2. How Data Is Used

Data is used solely to:
- Provide app functionality
- Store and sync user data
- Improve performance and stability

Personal data is **not sold** to third parties.

---

## 3. Cloud Services

The app may use third-party services such as Firebase for:
- Data storage
- Authentication
- Analytics

Availability depends on these services and internet connectivity.

---

## 4. Advertising

The free version may display ads via Google Mobile Ads.

Ads may use:
- Advertising ID
- Anonymous usage signals

Premium users may have ads removed.

---

## 5. Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are applied to protect data, but absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

---

## 6. Data Retention & Deletion

Users may:
- Delete data within the app (where available)
- Uninstall the app to stop data collection

Cloud data may persist until deleted or account removal.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Continued use indicates acceptance of changes.
